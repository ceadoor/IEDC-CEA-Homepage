ABOUT US

Innovation and Entrepreneurship Developmnet Centre at College of Engineering, Adoor(IEDC-CEA) functions with a purpose of inspiring students to become independent innovators and leaders by exposing then to the world of Entrepreneurship through Innovation. The centre encourages students to identify the true joy of self-learning and they passionately involve in bringing life into their ideas, to solve the problems that they see around.

Mission and Vision

To develop an ecosystem with required infrastructure that can enable students and faculty to innovate and prototype their potential ideas with industrial standards, expert assistance and support from Government institutions and help them to realize their potentials and thus make our world a better place to co-exist.

Objectives

1

To organise Entrepreneurship Awareness Camps, Entrepreneurship Development Programmes and Skill Development Programmes in the institution.

2

To guide and assist prospective entrepreneurs on various aspects such as preparing project reports, obtaining project approvals, loans and facilities from agencies of support system, information on technologies, etc.

3

To organize Business Plan Competitions every year.

4

To arrange interaction with entrepreneurs and create a mentorship scheme for student entrepreneurs.

5

To improve innovation, creative and design thinking among student community.

6

Focus more on innovation driven entrepreneurship from student projects.
